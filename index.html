Who We Are

This is the extended online version of Caliph Life School, which is India's first Life School which created a revolutionary change in education sector. At Caliph Virtual School, we are on a mission to revolutionize education by placing a strong emphasis on morals and values. We believe that education goes beyond academic achievements; it is about shaping individuals who are not only knowledgeable but also morally upright, compassionate, and responsible members of society.
